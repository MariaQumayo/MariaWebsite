Honours Research

How does the use of gameful design effect the intrinsic motivation of novice students learning the C++ programming language?

If students have a hard time learning something, it must mean that the thing they are learning is difficult to learn (Alam, Gumilang, and Hasibuan, n.d.; Bonar and Soloway 1985).

If educators hope to teach programming effectively, they must understand precisely what it is that makes learning how to program so difficult for so many students.

Lack Skills

***

Some reasons why novice students find programming difficult is that they lack the speciﬁc knowledge and skills of an expert, and this perspective pervades much of the literature (Antal 2000; Bonar and Soloway 1985).

Winslow (1996) conclude that novice students are limited to surface knowledge and organise knowledge based on superﬁcial similarities (Esteves et al. 2011)

Gameful Desgn

***

The aim of the study is to investigate the effects of gameful design, implemented through gamification, on intrinsic motivation for novice students to learn programming in C++.

The desired result of the study is for the effects of gamefule design to encourage and motivate students enough that they find within themselves the desire and enjoyment to learn programming well enough to succeed.

Gamification is not enough

***

They lack motivation to learn programming as they are confused, overwhelmed and feel incompetent (Deci, Koestner, and Ryan 2001). Gamification (the use of game design elements in nongame contexts.) is a popular approach for increasing students engagement (Deterding 2017).

The problem with gamification in educational systems, is that it encourages extrinsic motivation and creates extrinsically motivated students who come to class because they have to.

Qualitative Methodology

***

This study is of a qualitative nature and will be implemented using the qualitative research methodology.
Qualitative methodology gives way for the capture of aspects of social and psychological life (Mugenda 1999), such is the case with this study as it investigates the effects of one phenomena, gameful design, on intrinsic motivation..
